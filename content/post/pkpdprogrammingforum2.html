Abstract

After ISoP Data Standard working group published the Population PK Data Standard (ADPPK) on ISoP Data Standards Working Group in 2020, the working group has taken the standard to CDISC ADaM working group to make it CDISC compliant. In this presentation we will provide a status update and present the current CDSIC ADPPK Standard. We will also discuss how companies can adopt ADPPK and implement it into their workflows.

Presenter: Neelima Thanneer
